**Role:** Internal Audit, FinTech, Private Banking & Wealth Engineering
**Location:** Paris, France

A discovery internship across several departments at Rothschild & Co, spending the majority of the time embedded with the Internal Audit team while also gaining exposure to Private Banking, Wealth Engineering, and FinTech.

## Key Contributions
- Worked primarily within Internal Audit, shadowing audit directors and gaining visibility into how audits are run across the bank's different business lines.
- Spent time with teams in Private Banking and Wealth Engineering, learning how client-facing advisory work is structured at a private bank.
- Spent time in the FinTech department working alongside a Product Owner, getting an introduction to how digital products are scoped and managed inside a traditional banking institution.
- Built a broad, cross-departmental view of how a private bank operates, from oversight and risk in audit to client services and product development.

## Skills Developed
- Understanding of internal audit methodology and how it's applied across diverse business lines in a bank.
- Exposure to private banking and wealth engineering client service models.
- Insight into product management practices, gained via the Product Owner role, within a financial institution.
- Professional communication and adaptability across multiple teams and functions in a short period.
